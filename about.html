The Story

I created Iffrit Studio — and this site—to give my creative impulses a dedicated home beyond the constraints of day-to-day work. In a world of structured projects and deadlines, I craved a space to freely explore Gaming and RPG worlds, tinker with unconventional software ideas, and chase spontaneous design sparks without apology or expectation. This digital workshop captures those raw moments of inspiration and long sessions of work, turning fleeting thoughts into shareable artifacts that anyone curious about fantasy worlds, clever hacks, or joyful experimentation can discover and enjoy.
